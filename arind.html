Professor Arindam Basak, Assistant Professor

abasakfet@kiit.ac.in

Arindam Basak has 7+ years of experience in education and teaching. He had completed his graduation from the University of Kalyani, West Bengal and after that completed his post-graduation from Jadavpur University, West Bengal. He has completed his doctoral degree from the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology, Shibpur, West Bengal. His main research interest is Thin-film photovoltaics, sensor technology. He has authored a number of national and international journals. He is teaching courses on Control Systems, Power Plant Instrumentation, Measurement and Instrumentation, Basic Electronics, Electrical and Electronics Measurement, Analytical Instrumentation, Industrial Automation and Control. Apart from being a faculty, He is also the Associate Dean of Training and Placement for his department.
