Professor Sutanu Mangal, Assistant Professor

sutanufpy@kiit.ac.in

Dr. S. Mangal completed his PhD degree from IIT Kharagpur in 2011, during his PhD he had received EU Fellowship from Ministry of University, Govt of Italy. Later he joined in KIIT in 2011 as an assistant professor in department of physics. He did his post doc in Academia sinica in Taipei, Taiwan. Dr. S. Mangal has 7 years academic experiences and 10 years research experiences. His field of research topic is experimental condensed matter physics, semiconductor science and technology.
